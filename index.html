Featured Project

For my CAPSTONE PROJECT, I developed an e-commerce website designed specifically for artists and creatives looking to showcase and sell their work online. The platform was built with PHP and SQL on the backend to manage user authentication, artwork listings, shopping cart functionality, and secure transactions. On the frontend, I implemented responsive design with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to ensure the site was visually appealing and accessible across devices. The goal was to provide a user-friendly space where painters, illustrators, and digital artists could easily create profiles, upload their pieces, and engage with customers. This project allowed me to merge technical development with an appreciation for creativity, crafting a solution that empowers local talents to reach broader audiences.
